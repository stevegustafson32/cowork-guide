◇ Prompt Trainer

Learn a technique. Practice it live. See your score.

Pulled straight from The Prompting Guide. Each lesson teaches one tool, then drops you into a practice area that scores your prompt in real time.

The pattern is simple: teach → practice → score. No videos, no quizzes. Just one idea, one exercise, and honest feedback on the prompt you actually wrote.

  • 01
    Read the lessonA single prompting technique — role, constraints, few-shot, chain-of-thought — explained with one concrete example.
  • 02
    Do the exerciseA realistic task that requires the technique you just learned. Write it your way.
  • 03
    Get scoredClaude grades your prompt on clarity, specificity, role, constraints, and context — with honest notes on what to fix.
